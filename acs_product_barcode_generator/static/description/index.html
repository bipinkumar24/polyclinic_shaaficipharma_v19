ACS Product Barcode Generator.


In Normal odoo there is no option to auto generate Barcode numbers for your products. This module will add a functionality to allow barcode generation of EAN13 for products
You can do configuration at product or category or company level.
You will have to define the company default value the 6 next number the sequence.
The 13rd is the key of the EAN13, this will be automatically computed.
NOTE: This module generates EAN13 Barcode based on configuration on Sequence.

Product Barcode generation option on product.
Product Barcode generation configuration on product category.
Product Barcode generation configuration on company.
Generate Barcode for multiple Products at a time.
