ODOO POS Credit Limit

Credit Management with ease in Odoo POS!

POS Credit limit helps you manage the credit limit and hold for a customer. You can control how much credit a customer is allowed. After the credit limit is exhausted, the POS will show a warning message. Similarly, the POS will show various warning popups on credit hold and credit hold due to discounted products.

Below is the list of features Odoo POS Credit Limit

Configuring POS Credit Limit

  • Define the credit limit for a customer in the backend.
  • Enable setting to hold the credit limit of a customer.

Warning Popups

  • The module shows various popups like exceeding credit limit, credit hold, and when an order contains a discounted amount.

Displaying Credit Limit

  • The POS session shows the remaining credit limit of a customer in Odoo.

Add Credit Management in Odoo

  • The module adds a credit limit tab in the Odoo POS customer profile to define different limits for distinct customers.
Why Do You Need a POS Credit Limit?
POS credit limit helps manage the lenders of business, and you can check how much credit is pending. Also, it helps identify which user's credit limit need to be held and whose need to be updated. Further, it aids you in getting a hold of business revenue and debtors and helps manage the cash inflow and outflow.
Benefits of POS Credit Limit
  • The Odoo app helps identify the debtors (customers) of your business.
  • The module aids in managing the credit limit of every customer.
  • It allows you to hold the credit limit of customers.
  • It permits restraining customers from exceeding credit limits.
  • You can view the credit limit of a customer in Odoo POS.
Gives Competitive Edge to your Odoo POS
  • The module manages the customer's credit limit and gives you a competitive edge. It helps manage your finances as you know how much credit you have provided customers. Also, credit limit holding helps restrict customers from spending any amount of their credit limit.
  • The Odoo app also helps you track the customer's credit limit. You can set various criteria (pre-defined) in the backend for the credit limit.
