Detailed Description of Module

Hospital Commission Management By ACS
  • Give Commission to referring doctor.
  • Give Commission to Visiting doctor.
  • Give Commission to Third Party (Employee or Other).
  • Commission History.
  • Optional Commission option directly on invoice.
  • Commission Payment Invoice Creation in batch.
  • Option to give commission on specific amount (With fix percentage from contacts) or calculate it based on invoice lines from rules.
  • Option to Role Based commission with specific rules.
  • Option to manage commission to multiple Contacts at same time.
  • Manage Commission batch.
  • Manage Commission Rules & Roles based on product and product Categories.
