Certificate Management System


This module adds functionality to manage and issue certificates to partners, including employees, customers, and vendors. It maintains a complete history of certificate allocations, ensuring a structured and transparent certification process. Many companies recognize and reward individuals through various certificates, such as Employee of the Month, Employee of the Year, Star Performer, and other achievement-based recognitions. This module provides an efficient way to streamline certificate management, track issued certificates, and maintain historical records. It can be customized to fit different business models, making it a valuable tool for organizations looking to enhance employee engagement and stakeholder recognition.
