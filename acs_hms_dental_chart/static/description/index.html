Create and manage all HMS procedures directly from the chart view for easy tracking and updates.

Display detailed procedure information for each individual tooth, giving you clear insights into treatments performed.

The chart view is easily accessible from the patient profile, procedure forms, appointment scheduling, and treatment management sections, ensuring a seamless experience across the system.
